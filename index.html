About SafiScore

SafiScore is an alternative credit scoring and data analytics platform for Zimbabwe, leveraging mobile and web behavioral metadata. Our AI-driven technology helps financial institutions reduce defaults, expand approvals, and fight fraud—while including the underbanked.
