About Us

SafiScore is an alternative credit scoring and data analytics platform based on smartphone and web behavioural metadata. We make credit scoring more inclusive for underbanked and underserved Zimbabweans. With over a decade of experience and partnerships with major financial institutions, our solutions help reduce defaults, increase approval rates, and fight fraud.
