About SafiScore

Why Alternative Credit Scoring Works in Africa

1. Traditional Credit Gaps

Over 60% of adults in Sub-Saharan Africa are unbanked or lack formal credit history, according to World Bank data. Women, smallholder farmers, and informal traders are often excluded from traditional financial systems. Alternative credit scoring opens doors to fairer and more inclusive access to capital.

Read More →

2. Mobile-Driven Financial Behavior

With mobile penetration exceeding 90% in some regions, behavioral data like airtime top-ups, mobile money transfers, and WhatsApp usage offer deeper insights into financial habits. This data builds reliable credit profiles without requiring bank statements.

Read More →

3. Trust Without Banks

Alternative scoring empowers micro-lenders and fintechs to extend credit based on trust signals beyond banks. Community savings behavior, peer-to-peer payments, and smartphone activity can all validate creditworthiness—especially in rural and underserved regions.
