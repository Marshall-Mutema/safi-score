What is alternative credit scoring?
We use mobile payments, social, and behavioral data—with your consent—instead of only bank history, to build a fairer, more accurate score.
Who can access SafiScore?
Any Zimbabwean can sign up—especially if you lack bank credit history. Lenders, too, can access with user permission!
Is my data safe?
Yes. Data is encrypted and used only for scoring, with strict controls meeting Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe guidelines.
How do I improve my SafiScore?
Use EcoCash responsibly, pay lenders on time, and engage in positive digital habits. Our app will guide you with personalized tips!
