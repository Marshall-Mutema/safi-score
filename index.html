About Us

SafiScore is an alternative credit scoring and data analytics platform based on smartphone and web behavioural metadata available to any company and individuals with a mobile app and a website or social media account.

With over a decade of experience and several partnerships with major financial institutions, SafiScore makes credit scoring more inclusive towards underbanked and underserved Zimbabweans.

Our alternative credit scoring platforms and fraud detection solutions help financial institutions reduce defaults, increase approval rates, and combat fraud.
